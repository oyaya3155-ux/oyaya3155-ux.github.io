About Me

I am a solution-driven interaction designer specialising in vertical-scenario design across workplace, healthcare, and education domains. I excel at transforming complex requirements into intuitive, efficient, and implementable interaction solutions through rigorous user research and clear design logic, consistently seeking the optimal balance between user value and commercial feasibility.

My design process is centred on data-informed decision-making and iterative feedback, with end-to-end capabilities from user insight generation to high-fidelity prototype validation. I have led multiple innovative projects, including an award-winning medical support platform and a workplace efficiency tool addressing the needs of the 73.1% of professionals affected by overtime—both demonstrating my ability to resolve core pain points within real-world contexts. I believe that outstanding design should empower people—whether by offering warmth and support to families in medical contexts, enabling university students to achieve financial independence, or improving workplace efficiency and mental wellbeing. As a professional designer, I am committed to creating tangible commercial and user value through thoughtful interaction design.
